About Boston

Intro: Boston is the capital of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts in the United States, it is also the largest city in New England and the 24th largest city in the United States. One of the oldest cities in the United States, its rich history attracts many tourists. Also many colleges and universities make Boston an international center of higher education.

Travel Guide: The greater Boston area is a wonderful blend of stylish sophistication and historic New England charm. Attend one of our renowned events, uncover Boston's history by walking our famous Freedom Trail, dine in one of our new restaurants (or dine in an old favorite), shop anywhere from upscale boutiques on Newbury Street to premium outlet malls a short distance from downtown Boston, take a tour... the possibilities are endless!
