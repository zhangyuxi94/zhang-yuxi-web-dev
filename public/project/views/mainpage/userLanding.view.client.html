About Boston

Boston is the capital of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts in the United States, it is also the largest city in New England and the 24th largest city in the United States. One of the oldest cities in the United States, its rich history attracts many tourists. Also many colleges and universities make Boston an international center of higher education.
